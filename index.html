Why Glimpz?

According to our market research, music discovery is an active and engaging lean-forward activity, distinct from the passive and relaxed lean-back experience of music listening.

Auto previews, eye-pleasing UI: Effortlessly find new songs.

Portable playlists: Shareable across streaming platforms

Amplify artist campaigns, drive listens, and earn with PromoteLink

Our Vision: Make music discovery efficient and enjoyable, sprinkled with elements of serendipity, fandom, and coolness
